About Me

A brief introduction to my background and passion.

I am a senior HR technology strategist with a passion for transforming human resources through innovative data solutions. With a deep background in both product management and UI/UX design, I specialize in bridging the gap between complex HR challenges and elegant, user-centric applications. My work focuses on creating tools that not only solve problems but also empower leaders to make smarter, data-driven decisions about their most valuable asset: their people.

From designing comprehensive position management dashboards like ORG VISION to developing interactive workshop applications and authoring strategic roll-out plans, my experience is centered on delivering tangible business value. I thrive on turning high-level strategy into practical, maintainable, and impactful technology that enhances the employee experience and drives organizational success.
