Diagnostic: Key Drivers of Low HR Support Satisfaction

Long Resolution Time

Tickets related to Benefits & Leave of Absence take an average of 72 hours to resolve, driving down the overall score.

Low First-Contact Resolution

35% of employee inquiries require follow-up from the employee or escalation to another HR team member, indicating a lack of clear ownership or knowledge.

Lack of Proactive Communication

Survey comments frequently mention frustration with the lack of status updates on complex, multi-day tickets.

Prescriptive: Recommended Actions

Automate Onboarding Document Flow: Implement a workflow automation tool to manage onboarding document collection and verification, reducing manual effort by an estimated 50 hours per month.
Launch an HR Knowledge Base: Create and promote a centralized, searchable knowledge base for employees to self-serve answers to common benefits and policy questions, aiming to reduce ticket volume by 20%.
Review HR Service Delivery Model: Analyze ticket data to determine if a tiered support model (Tier 1 for simple inquiries, Tier 2 for complex cases) would improve resolution times and employee satisfaction.
