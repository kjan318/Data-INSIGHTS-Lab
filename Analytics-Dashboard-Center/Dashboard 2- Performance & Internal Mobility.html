    Lack of Career Paths: Data shows 65% of Engineering employees do not have a defined career path in the HRIS.

    Talent Hoarding: Exit surveys from high-performers in Sales frequently cite "no opportunity to move to another team."

    Low Role Visibility: Only 40% of open roles are posted internally before being advertised externally.
