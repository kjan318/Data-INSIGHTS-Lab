Prescriptive: Recommended Actions

Conduct Full Pay Equity Audit: Engage a third-party firm to conduct a privileged, comprehensive pay equity audit across gender and ethnicity, focusing on Engineering and Sales.
Market Salary Adjustment for Engineering: Implement a targeted 8-10% market salary adjustment for all roles in the Engineering department to address the competitiveness gap and mitigate retention risk.
Launch Benefits Education Campaign: Partner with the benefits provider to launch a targeted communication campaign to increase awareness and enrollment in the 401(k) plan, especially for employees under 30.
