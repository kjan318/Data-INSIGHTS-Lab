Diagnostic: Key Drivers of Declining Revenue per Employee

  • 1.
    High Vacancy in Revenue-Generating Roles: The 15% vacancy rate in Sales directly impacts revenue potential. Each open Account Executive role represents an estimated $1.5M in lost annual bookings.
  • 2.
    Slow Time to Productivity: New hires in Engineering take an average of 6 months to become fully productive, impacting product velocity and innovation.
  • 3.
    High-Performer Turnover: The 12% turnover rate for top-performing employees (see Retention dashboard) removes the most productive individuals from the workforce.

Prescriptive: Recommended Actions

Refine Forecasting Model: Incorporate leading indicators like voluntary turnover trends and sales pipeline data into the Q4 headcount forecasting model to improve accuracy.
Prioritize Critical Role Recruitment: Allocate two additional dedicated recruiters to focus exclusively on filling open roles in the Sales and Engineering departments.
Conduct Post-Mortem on Low-ROI Project: Launch a review of the Internal Mobility Platform to understand low adoption and determine a course-correction plan or decommissioning strategy.
